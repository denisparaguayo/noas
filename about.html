About Us

Noa Cleaning Service LLC is a professional cleaning company serving the DMV area. We are committed to providing exceptional cleaning services to our clients, ensuring their homes and businesses are clean, healthy, and welcoming.

Our Story

Our founder, Noa, immigrated to the United States from Paraguay over 20 years ago. She quickly realized the need for reliable and affordable cleaning services in the area. With a passion for cleanliness and a strong work ethic, Noa started her own cleaning company, providing exceptional service to her clients.

Over the years, Noa Cleaning Service LLC has grown into a reputable and trusted cleaning company. We have a team of experienced and dedicated cleaners who are committed to providing the highest quality of service. We use eco-friendly cleaning products and techniques to ensure the safety and health of our clients and their families.

Our Services

We offer a wide range of cleaning services, including:

  • House cleaning
  • Office cleaning
  • Post-construction cleaning
  • Move-in/move-out cleaning
  • Janitorial services

Our services are tailored to meet the specific needs of our clients. We offer flexible scheduling and competitive rates.

Our Commitment

We are committed to providing our clients with the highest quality of cleaning services. We are also committed to providing excellent customer service. We are always available to answer your questions and address your concerns.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide our clients with exceptional cleaning services that exceed their expectations. We want to make a difference in the lives of our clients by providing them with a clean and healthy environment.

Our Values

We are committed to providing our clients with the highest quality of service. We are also committed to being honest, trustworthy, and reliable. We believe in treating our clients with respect and dignity.

Our Team

Our team is made up of experienced and dedicated cleaners who are passionate about their work. We are committed to providing our clients with the best possible cleaning experience.

Our Guarantee

We guarantee your satisfaction with our services. If you are not satisfied with our work, we will return to your home or business and re-clean the area at no charge.
